Spagetti Recipe

Spagetti's are an easy to cook meal, and is incredibly convenient if you are looking to feed a large amount of people who are looking to eat something slightly elavorate.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1/2 onion
  • Chicken of beef
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the meat separetly and then chop it
  2. Chop the onion into small pieces
  3. Cook the onions
  4. Add tomato sauce and meat
  5. Let cook for 50 minutes
  6. Boil water, add salt and cook the spagetti
  7. Put the sauce on top of the spagetti
  8. Serve!
