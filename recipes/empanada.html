Empanada Recipe

Empanadas are a traditional argentinian dish which consists of a meet stuffing covered with a pastry, that can be done in the oven or fried. It is usually served as an entrance, but in some cases it can be served as the main dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 pastry for empanadas
  • 1/2kg of shredered meat
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1 tomato

Steps

  1. Cook the onion, garlic cloves and pepper in sunflower seed's oil
  2. Add shredered meat and mix until it's done
  3. Take it out of the fire and let cool down
  4. Put the stuffing into the pastry and close the pastry
  5. Put in the oven at max for 15 minutes
  6. Serve!
