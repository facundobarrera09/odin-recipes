Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a relatively simple dish consisting of "sheets" of pasta that contain various types of sauces and meats. A portion is generaly enough for one person, so making it can be really efective when cooking for many people.

Ingredients

  • 20oz of meat
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 peppers
  • 2 onions
  • 12 pasta sheets
  • ...

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta sheets for 10 minutes and then dry
  2. Prepare the meat stuffing
  3. Prepare bechamel sauce
  4. Put all the ingredients together, alternating between ingredient and pasta sheet
  5. Cook in the oven for 15 minutes at 180Â°C
  6. Grill for 2-5 minutes
  7. Serve!
